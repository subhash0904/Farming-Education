Managing Brown Root Disease in Trees: A Comprehensive Guide

Symptoms:

  • The affected roots are encrusted with a mass of soil and small stones held by a network of mycelium.
  • The affected taproot becomes rotted, and the entire tree is killed.

Management:

  • Removal and destruction of the infected trees.
  • Apply lime @ 2.5t/ha.
  • In the area of the dead tree, apply an additional dose of lime @ 25 kg/dead tree.
  • Wash the partially affected root with Emisan or Aretan 0.1%.
By implementing these comprehensive management strategies, you can effectively control and mitigate the impact of Brown Root Disease in your trees. Regular monitoring, early detection, and timely intervention are essential for maintaining a healthy and productive plantation.
