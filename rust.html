Rust: Uromyces phaseoli

Symptom:

Spots produced are small, numerous in number with a pale brown center and reddish-brown margin. Similar spots also occur on branches and pods. Under favorable environmental conditions, severe leaf spotting and defoliation occur at the time of flowering and pod formation. The fungus is seed-borne and also survives on plant debris in the soil. High humidity favors disease development.

Management:

Spray Mancozeb 1000g or wettable sulfur 1500g /ha at the initiation of the disease and 10 days later.
