Managing Corynespora Leaf Spot in Crops: A Comprehensive Guide

Symptoms:

  • Circular spots with brown or papery centers and with a dark brown ring appear on the young leaves.
  • A yellow halo develops around the ring. Short holes occur. Affected leaves dry up.

Management:

  • Spray with 1% Bordeaux mixture.
By implementing these comprehensive management strategies, you can effectively control and mitigate the impact of Corynespora Leaf Spot in your crops. Regular monitoring, early detection, and timely intervention are essential for maintaining a healthy and productive crop.
