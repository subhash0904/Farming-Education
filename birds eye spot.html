Managing Bird's Eye Spot in Crops: A Comprehensive Guide

Symptoms:

  • Circular spots with a grey center and with a brown border occur in large numbers in the nursery or on young plants in the field.

Management:

By implementing these comprehensive management strategies, you can effectively control and mitigate the impact of Bird's Eye Spot in your crops. Regular monitoring, early detection, and timely intervention are essential for maintaining a healthy and productive crop.
