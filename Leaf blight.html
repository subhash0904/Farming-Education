Disease symptoms Reddish brown oval spots appear on young seedlings with bright yellow margin. In severe cases, several spots coalesce to cause drying of leaves. It is a complex disease, having association of A. triticina, B. sorokiniana and A. alternate. Survival and spread Primary spread is by externally seed-borne and soil borne conidia.
